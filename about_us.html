(OI for short) is registered in Beijing Zhongguancun Science and Technology Park. It is a national high-tech enterprise dedicated to the field of electronic testing and maintenance. It is a member of the Zhongguancun Enterprise Credit Promotion Association and a new third board. Listed companies (Securities abbreviation: Ocean Stocks, stock code: 839145). In the field of electronic testing, Ocean Instruments is a leading comprehensive service provider in the industry. It can provide 4S comprehensive services including sales, system integration, technology development, and maintenance of electronic test and measurement instruments. The company's customers are mainly colleges, universities, National defense industry, scientific research units, and electronic manufacturing related companies. The company's products and services are widely used in communications, information technology, electrical and industrial process control and other fields.
